## 1. one-surface — min 10s

This is Sim — one workspace. Everything your system uses lives here, and each piece has a name: a Mothership, a table, a knowledge base, a workflow, a file, a tool, logs, a deployment.

## 2. two-kinds — min 11s

There are two kinds of things in here. Resources — tables, knowledge bases, files — hold what your system knows. Workflows do the work, running over what those resources hold.

## 3. wired-together — min 14s

An AI system is just a workflow wired to your resources. You describe what you want in plain language, and the Mothership builds it — pulling from your tables and knowledge bases, then acting, shipping, and keeping a record of every run.

## 4. the-series — min 11s

Each of these is a stop on this series — a short video apiece. The Mothership, workflows, tables, knowledge bases, files, tools, deployments, logs. You don't need to know any of it yet.

## 5. into-the-workflow — min 14s

Workflows are where we start. In the next video, we go inside one and build it from scratch — a Start block, an agent, and a response — the same chain that classifies a customer message.
